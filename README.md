## Problem Statement
Renewable energy generation is inherently variable. City-scale energy planning
requires accurate forecasting and explainable insights to ensure grid stability
and maximize clean energy utilization.

## Objective
GridSense provides short-term solar energy generation forecasts using weather data,
enabling planners to anticipate variability and make informed decisions.

## Scope
- Solar energy forecasting
- Weather-driven estimation
- Decision-support analytics

## Out of Scope
- Real-time grid control
- IoT device management
- Financial settlement systems
